An extension which blocks distractions and allows you to focus on your tasks.
This is the Homepage of Focus. By pressing the Focus button you can start your focus session. The UnFocus Button allows you to stop your focus session and the setting button redirects you to the options page where you can customize your extension.

This is the Options Page for the Extension.

Websites To Block

Enter the title of the websites you need to block for the focus session. You can add textboxes with the '+' button and remove them using the 'x' button.

Focus Time

Enter the length of your focus session in minutes.

Microbreaks

Long sessions at the computer tire our eyes and increase our fatigue. By switching on this option, the extension will periodically remind you to take micro breaks in order to keep your eyes healthy.
